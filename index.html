Questions?

How long does delivery actually take?

15–30 minutes from the moment your payment clears, depending on how far you are from our launch point at 6350 Turtlemound Rd. Flagler Ave drop zones are typically 15 min. Klondike and Smyrna Dunes are closer to 25–30 min. We'll text you an ETA once your order is confirmed.

Do I need to stand in a specific spot?

Nope! Just be near your chosen drop zone when we text you "it's landing!" The drone lands briefly at the boardwalk or access point — you pick up the package (usually a 30-second walk) and we're gone. No awkward hovering over strangers.

Is this for real? Is it legal?

100% real. Shore Drop operates under FAA Part 107 Remote Pilot certification. Under Florida F.S. 330.41, the FAA — not local governments — regulates drone delivery operations. We're the first commercial beach drone delivery service on the Volusia County coast.

What if the weather is bad?

Florida afternoons happen. If a storm rolls in after your order, we'll refund you immediately — no questions asked. We monitor NWS Daytona Beach radar constantly. The SwellPro drone handles wind up to 38 mph, so light chop is no problem; lightning grounds us.

What payment methods do you accept?

We use Stripe for secure checkout. Credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover), Apple Pay, and Google Pay all work. Tap "Order Now" to go straight to a secure payment page — your card info never touches our servers.

Can I get alcohol delivered?

Not yet — we're working on our Florida alcohol beverage license. Coming in 2027. For now: everything else you need for a great day at the beach!

What's your phone number / can I text you?

Yes! After placing your order you'll get a text from our ops line. You can reply to that with any questions or last-minute instructions. Pre-launch questions: mistertichenor@gmail.com
